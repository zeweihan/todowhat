# SumiDay — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-04-16
**Last updated:** 2026-04-16

---

SumiDay is built on a simple conviction: your inner life is yours. We designed this Privacy Policy — and the product itself — to collect only what we genuinely need to run the service, protect what we do collect, and be transparent about everything.

This Policy applies to the SumiDay mobile application ("App") and website at sumi.day ("Site"), operated by **Zhen Shan Mei Grace Limited**, a company incorporated in Hong Kong ("SumiDay", "we", "our", "us").

## 1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Policy applies to anyone who:
- Downloads, installs, or uses the SumiDay App.
- Visits sumi.day.
- Contacts us by email or any other means.

SumiDay is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age threshold in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at **legal@sumi.day** and we will promptly delete it.

## 2. What Data We Collect and Why

We collect only the minimum necessary. Here is everything:

### 2.1 Account Data

| Data | What it is | Why we collect it |
|---|---|---|
| Email address | The email you register with (if not using Sign in with Apple) | Account creation, login, service communications |
| Apple ID token | Opaque token issued by Apple if you use Sign in with Apple | Authenticate you without us ever seeing your Apple credentials |
| User ID | A unique internal identifier (UUID) generated by Supabase | Link your data across the service |

When you use **Sign in with Apple**, Apple may share a relay email address (a private alias) rather than your real email. We respect that choice and operate with whatever Apple provides.

### 2.2 Subscription and Entitlement Data

| Data | What it is | Why we collect it |
|---|---|---|
| RevenueCat customer ID | Anonymous identifier for your subscription state | Verify whether you have an active subscription |
| Purchase receipt (Apple) | Receipt processed by RevenueCat on your behalf | Unlock premium features; detect fraud |

We never see or store your credit card or payment information. All billing is handled by Apple.

### 2.3 Content Data (Your Reflections)

| Data | What it is | Why we collect it |
|---|---|---|
| Written reflections | Text you type in response to daily prompts | Store your journal history and display it to you |
| Voice reflections | Audio recordings (if you use voice input) | Transcribe and store your reflection with your consent |
| Practice session log | Which passages you engaged with, dates | Power your streak, history, and progress views |

Your Reflections are private. We do not use your Reflections to train AI models, sell them to advertisers, or share them with any third party except as required to deliver the service to you (see Section 5).

### 2.4 AI Dialogue Data

When you engage with the AI sage dialogue feature, the text of your conversation (your question and the App's response) is processed via our AI API providers (see Section 5) in real time to generate the response. We do not store the full dialogue transcript on our servers beyond what is needed for the in-session experience, except for any portion you explicitly save as a reflection.

We use anonymised versions of interactions (stripped of all identifying information) to monitor output quality and safety. We do not use your conversations to fine-tune models.

### 2.5 Analytics Data

We use **TelemetryDeck**, a privacy-first analytics service. TelemetryDeck is designed to be GDPR-compliant and does not collect personally identifiable information (PII). It uses hashed, double-blind user identifiers.

| Data | What it is | Why we collect it |
|---|---|---|
| App version, iOS version | Technical environment | Identify compatibility issues |
| Feature usage signals | Which screens/features are used (no content) | Understand which features are valuable |
| Crash reports | Stack traces on crash | Fix bugs |

We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs (Facebook, Google, etc.). There are no tracking pixels in the App.

### 2.6 Support and Communications

If you contact us by email, we retain the content of that communication and your email address to respond to your enquiry and to keep a record of issues.

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Account data (email, user ID) | For the life of your account + 30 days after deletion |
| Reflections and practice logs | For the life of your account; deleted within 30 days of account deletion |
| AI dialogue session data | Retained in-session only; not stored on our servers after the session ends |
| Support emails | 3 years from last interaction |
| Analytics signals (TelemetryDeck) | Rolling 12 months |
| Subscription/entitlement data (RevenueCat) | As required for tax/accounting compliance (generally 7 years) under Hong Kong law |

When you delete your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it (e.g., financial records).

## 5. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We share personal data only with the service providers listed below, under written data processing agreements where required, and only to the extent necessary to run SumiDay. We do not sell your personal data.

### Supabase
**Role:** Database and authentication provider.
**Data shared:** Email/user ID, all stored user data (reflections, practice logs).
**Where processed:** Supabase cloud infrastructure (AWS). Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
**Data processing agreement:** In place.
**Privacy policy:** https://supabase.com/privacy

### Anthropic (Claude API) / OpenAI
**Role:** AI inference for sage dialogue and content generation.
**Data shared:** The text of your in-session query and relevant context (passage being discussed). No account data or email is included in API calls.
**Where processed:** Anthropic / OpenAI servers (US).
**Data processing agreement:** In place with both providers under their enterprise/API terms.
**Anthropic Privacy Policy:** https://www.anthropic.com/privacy
**OpenAI Privacy Policy:** https://openai.com/privacy

*Note: We may also use additional AI API providers (such as Kimi, Mimo, or DeepSeek) for fallback inference. The same data minimisation principles apply: only the in-session query text is sent, with no account identifiers.*

### ElevenLabs
**Role:** Text-to-speech audio generation for daily practice sessions.
**Data shared:** The text of the audio script (pre-written content, not user data). No personal information is sent to ElevenLabs.
**Privacy policy:** https://elevenlabs.io/privacy

### RevenueCat
**Role:** Subscription entitlement management.
**Data shared:** Your RevenueCat anonymous customer ID and Apple receipt data (no payment card information).
**Where processed:** RevenueCat servers (US).
**Privacy policy:** https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### TelemetryDeck
**Role:** Privacy-first analytics.
**Data shared:** Hashed, double-blind device signals. No email, no name, no reflections content.
**Where processed:** TelemetryDeck servers (EU).
**Privacy policy:** https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy

### Apple Inc.
**Role:** App distribution, in-app purchases, Sign in with Apple.
**Data shared:** Apple manages the App Store relationship and billing. When you use Sign in with Apple, Apple processes your identity under their own privacy policy.
**Privacy policy:** https://www.apple.com/privacy/

### Legal Disclosures
We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of SumiDay, our users, or the public.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### All Users
- **Access:** Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correction:** Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Deletion:** Ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten"). The easiest way is to use the "Delete Account" feature in App Settings, which triggers an automatic deletion workflow.
- **Data export:** Request an export of your Reflections and practice log in a portable format.

### EU / EEA / UK Users (GDPR / UK GDPR)
In addition to the above, you have the right to:
- **Restriction:** Ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
- **Objection:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw consent:** Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
- **Lodge a complaint:** With your local supervisory authority (e.g., your national data protection authority in the EU, or the ICO in the UK). We would, however, appreciate the chance to address your concerns first.

### California Users (CCPA / CPRA)
California residents have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell (we do **not** sell personal information).
- Delete personal information we hold about you.
- Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do **not** engage in such practices).
- Non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

**Designated method to submit requests:** Email **legal@sumi.day** with the subject line "Privacy Request" and your registered email address.

### Users in Mainland China (PIPL)
If you are located in Mainland China (noting that the App is not currently available in Mainland China but may be in future versions), you have the following rights under the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL):
- Know how your personal information is processed.
- Access, copy, correct, and delete your personal information.
- Object to and restrict the processing of your personal information.
- Transfer your personal information to other platforms.
- Request an explanation of our personal information processing rules.

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures appropriate to the nature of the data we process, including:
- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses TLS 1.2+.
- **Encryption at rest:** Data stored in Supabase is encrypted at rest.
- **Access controls:** Only authorised personnel have access to personal data, on a need-to-know basis.
- **Authentication:** We use Supabase Auth with secure token handling. Sign in with Apple avoids passwords entirely.

No security system is perfect. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant supervisory authorities as required by applicable law.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SumiDay is a **native iOS application**. We do not use browser cookies. The App uses Apple's standard local storage and keychain for session tokens and preferences. TelemetryDeck uses a privacy-safe hashed identifier approach that does not track you across apps or websites.
