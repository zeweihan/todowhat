### Full Legal Disclaimer

**Nature of the Service.** SumiDay is a philosophical practice and educational application. All content in the App — including but not limited to curated classical passages, AI-generated commentary, Socratic prompts, sage persona dialogues, audio introductions, and user interface copy — is provided for **educational, reflective, and personal development purposes only**.

**Not a Healthcare Service.** SumiDay is not a mental health service, therapy service, counselling service, psychotherapy service, coaching service, or medical service of any kind. The App does not provide diagnosis, treatment, cure, mitigation, or prevention of any mental health condition, psychological disorder, emotional disturbance, or physical illness.

**Not Professional Advice.** Nothing in the App constitutes — or should be construed as — professional mental health advice, psychological advice, psychiatric advice, medical advice, legal advice, financial advice, or any other form of licensed professional advice. The AI-generated content produced within the App (including responses generated by the sage dialogue system) is not the output of a licensed therapist, counsellor, psychologist, physician, or other regulated professional.

**Philosophical Content.** The traditions and authors represented in SumiDay — including Stoicism, Zen Buddhism, Taoism, Confucianism, and related philosophical schools — offer frameworks for reflection and self-cultivation. Engaging with these traditions through SumiDay may be personally meaningful or enriching. It is not a substitute for professional care.

**Seek Professional Help When Needed.** If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, thoughts of self-harm or suicide, symptoms of a psychological or psychiatric condition, or any other situation requiring professional care, please contact a qualified mental health professional, your primary care physician, or an appropriate emergency service immediately. Do not delay seeking professional help because of content you have encountered in the App.

**Emergency Resources.** In a crisis, please contact:
- **International Association for Suicide Prevention** — https://www.iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres/
- **Crisis Text Line (US/UK/Canada/Ireland)** — Text HOME to 741741 (US) or 85258 (UK)
- **Your local emergency services** — (e.g., 999 in UK/HK, 911 in US/Canada)

**No Endorsement.** Reference to specific philosophers, schools of thought, texts, or traditions does not constitute an endorsement of those traditions as a treatment for any condition, nor does it imply that SumiDay has been certified, approved, or reviewed by any religious, philosophical, or medical authority.

**User Acknowledgement.** By using SumiDay, you acknowledge that you have read this disclaimer, that you understand SumiDay is not a healthcare or mental health service, and that you will seek appropriate professional help if you need it.

## Part D — Content Safety Guidelines for AI Dialogue

The sage dialogue system must be configured to:

1. **Never simulate a licensed professional.** If a user asks "are you a therapist?" or "can you treat my anxiety?", the AI must clearly decline that role and redirect to professional support.
2. **Detect crisis signals.** If a user message contains high-confidence indicators of self-harm ideation, suicidal ideation, acute distress, or abuse, the AI response must include the Version 4 crisis prompt and a reference to emergency resources, and must not engage philosophically with the crisis content.
3. **Stay in tradition.** Sage responses draw only from the relevant philosophical tradition's texts and concepts — they never prescribe medication, offer diagnoses, or provide therapeutic techniques.
4. **Decline medical questions.** If asked about medication, diagnoses, psychiatric symptoms, or medical conditions, the AI should politely note that this falls outside its role and suggest the user speak with a qualified professional.
