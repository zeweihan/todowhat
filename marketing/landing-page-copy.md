**[SECTION LABEL — Matcha — small caps — tracked]**  
A NOTE ON DEPTH

### The world has enough apps that help you feel better.

---

The guided meditation has its place. So does the breathing exercise, the gratitude prompt, the sleep story. These are real things that help real people.

But there is a different category of practice — one that does not offer comfort so much as it offers confrontation. A text that asks something of you. A question that does not resolve into advice.

---

**[PULL QUOTE — display type — 22px — Washi — thin Persimmon rule above and below]**

Marcus Aurelius did not write to feel better.  
He wrote to hold himself to account.

---

Zhuangzi did not teach to reduce anxiety. He asked questions that dissolved the assumptions behind the anxiety. The Analects are not a leadership framework. They are a lifelong argument about what it means to become a person.

This tradition is available to anyone willing to spend ten minutes with it. Every day. Without rushing.

That is what SumiDay is.

## GM NOTES FOR CHAIRMAN REVIEW

1. **One open item before design can begin**: tagline selection. Both options appear wherever the tagline is rendered — mark your choice (A or B) and I will produce a clean version with all instances resolved.

2. **Section 3 (Why classical wisdom?)** is intentionally the most literary section on the page. If A/B testing reveals a scroll drop-off, the safe cut is to remove the second body paragraph and keep only the pull quote + final three sentences. The emotional core is preserved.

3. **Section 5 (Social proof)** is fully a placeholder. My recommendation: suppress the counter entirely at launch if the number is below 200, and add the first real testimonials from TestFlight in Week 11–12 (per the phase roadmap). A small, honest number is worse than no number.

4. **ES version** mirrors this structure exactly. Ready to produce once the EN version is locked.

5. **Pricing is not on this page by design.** Pre-launch landing pages with pricing before product exists tend to create objections, not conversions. Price will appear in the App Store listing and in the onboarding paywall. If Chairman disagrees, I can add a "Pricing" section above the footer.
